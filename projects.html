Image Compressor

I wrote a jpeg-like image compressor and decompressor in C. The program transforms an image from the RGB color space to the component video (Y, Pb, Pr), then with discrete cosign transformation to a, b, c, and d values. Finally it trims b, c, and d to the save space and packs it into a file format. The decompressor does this process in reverse. Message or email me to discuss this or see source code.

Electric Car

I am an active participant on the Tufts Electric Racing team. We won 4th in 2019 and 6th in 2018 at the Formula Hybrid Competition, all electric category. I have led teams in designing important parts of the car.

My freshman year, I participated in a complete redesign of the suspension system. Our new suspension system was a resounding success, keeping the car several inches off the ground was also providing a comfortable ride.

I also led a group that designed the fiberglass composite side pannel covers. I also assisted the team that designed the new nose cone. We did all the design, molds, layups, and attachment in house.

My freshman year, I learned how to assemble the cooling system. I designed a custom mount for the pump and attachments for the radiator. Then, my sophmore year, I led the maintanace of our car's cooling system.

My sophmore year, I led a team that redesigned the seat tub, creating a new, better looking and more comfortable riding experience. Our new seat has new alcantara covered cushions and an improved design for easier driver egress.

I also started participating in the electrical side of the car my sophmore year. I did general work like wiring and crimping. Additionally, I also handled mechanical-like electrical tasks. For example, I redesigned the battery mount to be more secure, usable, and robust. I also was tasked with mounting an odd solenoid and grounding all metal components to the chassis.

Tufts Bikes

I am vice president for Tufts' community bike share. We maintain a fleet of around 30 bikes that students can check out from the library for free. In addition, we also offer weekly mechanics hours where we help members of the community fix their bikes. I was treasurer starting my freshman year, doubling the budget, then nearly a doubling it again my sophmore year. Starting my sophmore year, I was a shift lead, the most senior and responsible mechanic for one of the mechanics hours. I opened the shop, fixed bikes, and taught new mechanics how to fix bikes.
